This manifest details the entire contents of this Student Robotics kit box. All parts marked with an asterisk must be returned to Student Robotics immediately after the competition, unless other arrangements have been made in advance.

Be aware that this kit contains a Lithium Polymer battery (a LiPo). Handled correctly, they are a useful battery with excellent energy density. Handled incorrectly, they are potentially dangerous.

LiPos must be securely attached when in use on the robot, and care must be taken when charging and storing them. They should be kept in the charging bags when being charged, and in either the charging bags or equivalent secure storage when not in use.

In addition to this Kit Box, you should have received two 1m lengths of M6 threaded bar, four 2m lengths of aluminium L section and two token boxes.
